Combat Skills

  • The following Skills involve fighting in combat.

    • Attack

  • Grants players using melee weapons higher accuracy
  • Stronger melee weapons require a higher Attack Level in order to equip.
  • Also grants access to more powerful Attack related abilities.

    • Strength

  • Increases the players Maximum Hit in melee combat.
  • Some melee weapons may also require Strength levels to equip.
  • Strength works with Agility, and gives players access to certain Shortcuts.
  • Levelling Strength grants access to more powerful Strength related abilities.

    • Defence

  • Allows players to wear stronger Armour and equipment.
  • Grants the player access to Defensive abilities.

    • Ranged

  • Allows the player to fight from a distance via Arrows or other Physical Projectiles.
  • Increases the players chance to hit when using Physical Ranged attacks.
  • Grants access to higher levelled Ranged weapons and Armours.
  • Grants access to varying Agility Shortcuts (also requires Agility level).
  • Gives access to powerful Ranged abilities in combat.

    • Magic

  • Allows the player to cast spells, including Teleports and Enchantments.
  • Increases Magic-based attack damage dealt, and reduces Magic-based damage taken.
  • Gives access to varying powerful Magic related Abiliteis.

    • Constitution

  • Allows the player to sustain more damage without dying.
  • A players base Lifepoints are equal to 100 times their constitution level.
  • Grants access to Constitution based abilities (mainly healing related).
