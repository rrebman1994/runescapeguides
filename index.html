Combat Skills

  • The following Skills involve fighting in combat.

    • Attack

  • Grants players using melee weapons higher accuracy
  • Stronger melee weapons require a higher Attack Level in order to equip.
  • Also grants access to more powerful Attack related abilities.

    • Strength

  • Increases the players Maximum Hit in melee combat.
  • Some melee weapons may also require Strength levels to equip.
  • Strength works with Agility, and gives players access to certain Shortcuts.
  • Levelling Strength grants access to more powerful Strength related abilities.

    • Defence

  • Allows players to wear stronger Armour and equipment.
  • Grants the player access to Defensive abilities.

    • Ranged

  • Allows the player to fight from a distance via Arrows or other Physical Projectiles.
  • Increases the players chance to hit when using Physical Ranged attacks.
  • Grants access to higher levelled Ranged weapons and Armours.
  • Grants access to varying Agility Shortcuts (also requires Agility level).
  • Gives access to powerful Ranged abilities in combat.

    • Magic

  • Allows the player to cast spells, including Teleports and Enchantments.
  • Increases Magic-based attack damage dealt, and reduces Magic-based damage taken.
  • Gives access to varying powerful Magic related Abiliteis.

    • Constitution

  • Allows the player to sustain more damage without dying.
  • A players base Lifepoints are equal to 100 times their constitution level.
  • Grants access to Constitution based abilities (mainly healing related).

    • Prayer

  • Allows the use of Prayer assistance, such as stat boosts and partial immunity to damage types.
  • Effects last until the player runs out of Prayer Points, or the player manually turns them off.
  • Higher Prayer levels also grants the player access to certain armour types.

    • Summoning

  • Allows the player to summon pets and familiars for both combat and non-combat assistance.
  • Certain familiars will hold items for the player, allowing the ability to exceed an inventory cap of 28 items.

    Gathering Skills

  • The following skills involve gathering raw materials directly from the source.

    • Mining

  • Allows the player to gather ores and gems from rocks foung in varying locations.
  • Ores can be used with the Smithing Skill.
  • Gems can be used with the Crafting Skill.

    • Fishing

  • Allows the Player to catch varying types of Fish.
  • Fish can be cooked and eaten to restore HP (Hit Points).
  • Fish can also be sold to other players or shops.

    • Woodcutting

  • Allows the player to cut down trees for logs, or carve canoes out of certain trees.
  • Logs can be used with Fletching, Firemaking, or sold to other players or shops.

    • Farming

  • Allows the player to plant and harvest varying flora across the world.
  • Most notably used to farm Herbs for the Herblore skill.

    • Hunter

  • Allows players to Trap, Snare, Deadfall, Track, and Net animals for their hides, abilities and treasures.
  • Some animals, like Chinchompas and Salamanders, can be used as weapons.

    • Divination

  • Allows players to collect divine energies around the world to create powerful signs and portents.

    • -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Artisan Skills

  • The following skills involve using resource items to create, generally better, items.

    • Herblore

  • Allows the player to create unique potions and poisons to aid with skilling, and killing.
  • The higher the players level in Herblore, the more powerful their potions become.

    • Crafting

  • Allows players to create items from raw materials such as Pottery, Ranged Armours, and Jewellery.

    • Fletching

  • Allows the player to create projectile weapons and ammosuch as Bows, Arrows, Bolts, and Darts.

    • Smithing

  • Allows the player to smelt Ore into bars, used for crafting armours, weapons, and other useful items.

    • Cooking

  • Cooking allows the player to cook food.
  • Food is used to restore the players HP (Hit Points).
  • The higher the players level is in cooking, the less likely they are to burn food.

    • Firemaking

  • Allows players to light fires, Laterns, and Candles.
  • These fires can also be used to cook food!

    • Runecrafting

  • Allows the player to craft runes, useful for Magic skill.

    • Construction

  • Allows the player to build their very own house!
  • One of the more valuable aspects of a PoH (Player-Owned House) is the use of Gilded Altars, for Massive Prayer bonuses.

    • -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Support Skills

  • The following skills help the player in various ways.

    • Agility

  • Agility allows the use of most, if not all shortcuts in the game.
  • This skill increases the rate at which players recover run energy, and decreases the rate at which it drains.
  • Higher levels of Agility unlock unique perks for other stats such as Fishing and Thieving!

    • Thieving

  • Allows the player to steal from Market Stalls, chests, and certain NPCs (Non-Player Characters).

    • Slayer

  • Allows the player to kill monsters using tatics not used in normal combat.
  • Very useful skill for levelling combat stats, and can give some very valuable drops.

    • Dungeoneering

  • Allows the player to delve into dungeons unique to the corner of the world it's in.
  • Gives access to unique rewards only obtainable through Dungeoneering.
  • Also allows the player to access unique skilling locations around the world.

    • -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Elite Skills

  • The only Elite Skill in the game at the moment is the following.
  • Elite Skills don't replace regular skills, but are meant to coexist.

    • Invention

  • Invention allows the player to disassemble many of the items in the game to their core components.
  • The components can then be used to create new items, and upgrade existing items.
