Common Weapon Types

Below are some of the more common types of weapons used through the Attack Skill.

  • Bronze: Weakest possible weapons available. Level 1
  • Iron: Stronger than Bronze. Level 10
  • Steel: Stronger than Iron. Level 20
  • Corrupt Ancient Warriors' (M): Consists of several Degradable weapons, each only lasting 15 minutes
  • Black: Stronger than Steel. Cannot be made via Smithing skill. Level 25
  • White (M): Shares the same combat stats with Black weapons, but provides a small Prayer bonus. Cannot be made via Smithing. Level 25
  • Mithril: Stronger than Black and White weapons. Level 30
  • Adamant: Stronger than Mithril. Level 40
  • Rune: Stronger than Adamant. Level 50
  • Sacred Clay (M): Shares the same combat stats as Rune. Obtainable through Stealing Creation Minigame. Level 50
  • Gravite: Obtained through Dungeoneering skill for 40,000 Tokens. Lasts for 10 hours in combat. Level 55
  • Granite (M): Granite Maul (2-hand) and Granite Mace (1-hand) only provide Crush attacks. Level 55 Attack AND Strength
  • Obsidian (M): Stronger than Rune. All require Level 60 Attack except for Tzhaar-Ket-Om Level 60 Strength
  • Dragon (M): Stronger than Rune. Level 60
  • Dragon (Corrupt): Provides same stats as Dragon, but breaks down after 30 minutes of use. Level 60
  • Barrows (M): Features item sets worn by 6 minibosses. When the player wears a full set, it has a chance to activate a "Set Bonus". Level 70
  • Abyssal Whip (M): Very powerful 1-hand weapon with an attack speed as fast as Scimitars. Level 70
  • God Wars Equipment (M): Strongest non-degradable weapons in the game. All similar combat stats, but each has a Unique special ability. Level 75
  • Ancient Warriors' (M): Powerful 1-hand items that last for 1 hour in combat before degrading to dust. Level 78
  • Chaotic Weaponry (M): Obtained through Dungeoneering skill for 200,000 Tokens. Lasts for 10 hours in combat. Level 80
  • Tetsu (M): Can be made from resources in Player-Owned Ports. Lasts for 10 hours before needing to be recharged. Level 85
  • Drygore (M): Main and Off-hand are possible drops from the Kalphite King. Can be repaired using Chitin Scraps. Level 90
