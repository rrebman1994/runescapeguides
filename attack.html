Common Weapon Types

  • Bronze: Weakest possible weapons available. Level 1
  • Iron: Stronger than Bronze. Level 10
  • Steel: Stronger than Iron. Level 20
  • Corrupt Ancient Warriors': Consists of several Degradable weapons, each only lasting 15 minutes
  • Black: Stronger than Steel. Cannot be made via Smithing skill. Level 25
  • White: Shares the same combat stats with Black weapons, but provides a small Prayer bonus. Cannot be made via Smithing. Level 25
  • Mithril: Stronger than Black and White weapons. Level 30
  • Adamant: Stronger than Mithril. Level 40
  • Rune: Stronger than Adamant. Level 50
  • Sacred Clay: Shares the same combat stats as Rune. Obtainable through Stealing Creation Minigame. Level 50
